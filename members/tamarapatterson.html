Tamara Patterson

Mountain View, CA, USA

Cohort 2+

Passionate about building meaningful solutions, I thrive on the intersection of technology and human experience. After graduating from Caltech, I became the go-to person for data-driven insights at a Johnson & Johnson company. Discussing with business leaders to identify actionable data, I built complex queries that drove results.

Driven by my MBA, I transitioned to a startup, where I embraced the entire product lifecycle. In just two years, I progressed from an individual contributor to a people manager and eventually became the head of customer and product. During this time, I made pivotal contributions to a new product that generated a remarkable 20% increase in revenue with a key client.

Returning to my tech roots, as a full-stack software engineer I am dedicated to delivering impactful user experiences. Recently, I developed a JavaScript app that helps busy individuals discover new movies quickly, saving them valuable time.

As a passionate connector of technology and humanity, I find immense fulfillment in my role as a full-stack software engineer. Let's build something remarkable together.
