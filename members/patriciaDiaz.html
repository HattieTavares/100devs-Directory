Patricia Diaz

New York, NY - United States

Cohort 2

I'm a Full-Stack Software Developer based in New York City. After graduating from college with a BA in Psychology, I worked in customer service and health care coordination.

Due to the COVID-19 Pandemic, people's lives were reshaped and digital platforms were used to continue with our daily functions during lockdown. This is when I got motivated about coding and created a web app that helped the users get updated information about COVID-19 and provided a guide about local resources. In those uncertain times, it was when I completely fell in love with coding because I was able to build apps to solve problems and improve people's lives.
